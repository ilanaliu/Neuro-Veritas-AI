Labs

Neuro Veritas Labs is a student founded tech startup focused on developing AI-driven speech analysis tools to detect early signs of dementia across multiple languages. By combining linguistic patterns and logic testing, the lab aims to revolutionize early diagnosis and cognitive health monitoring through accessible, non-invasive technology..
