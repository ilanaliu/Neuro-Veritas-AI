Hi there! My name is Ilana Liu, and I’m a junior at North Haven High School with a strong passion for biomedical engineering and data science. As a first-generation Asian American student, I’m deeply engaged in STEM through academic research, dual enrollment in advanced college courses like Multivariable Calculus and Applied Data Science, and by founding a town-wide STEM outreach club to inspire younger students. I’ve conducted neuroscience research under a Yale professor. In addition, I’ve volunteered extensively at Yale New Haven Hospital. Beyond academics, I’m a competitive fencer, math team leader, musician, and committed volunteer tutor. My long-term goal is to pursue a dual degree in Biomedical Engineering and Finance to drive innovation in healthcare while shaping global economic strategy.
