Our Research

Our research at Neuro Veritas AI started with a goal to create a tool that could help detect early signs of memory loss through speech, no matter the language someone speaks. We began by studying how memory-related conditions like dementia show up in speech patterns and explored ways to use artificial intelligence to recognize those signs. After gathering voice samples in different languages and preparing the data, we built a model that listens for subtle changes in how people speak. The project combines science, technology, and global accessibility to support early intervention and improve brain health worldwide.
